### What are databases?
<p>Databases are used to store data and organize related information. Cloud engineers use databases to store data on a server in case of hardware restrictions or to use the services provided to manage their infrastructure.</p>

### What do database professionals do?
<p>Database professionals are responsible for organizing and visualizing relations in data to create information used for analysis. The most interesting part of their job is systemizing information and ensuring data security.</p>
